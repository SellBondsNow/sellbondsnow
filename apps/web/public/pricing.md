# sellbonds.now pricing

**Free to use. Zero protocol fee.**

There are no plans, no tiers, and no accounts. sellbonds.now is direct-to-chain and non-custodial — there is nothing to subscribe to.

## What you pay
- **Protocol fee:** $0. No fee on issuance, funding, drawdown, or repayment. sellbonds.now never takes a cut of principal or coupon.
- **On-chain gas:** you pay your own gas, signed from your local wallet.
- **Mainnet:** bring your own USDC and your own gas (ETH). Same flow, no dispenser.
- **Testnet (Base Sepolia):** gas (ETH) and test USDC are dispensed free by the sellbonds.now dispenser.

## What there isn't
- No rate limits, no usage quotas, no API keys, no seats.
- No custom domains, no webhooks, no SLAs to buy.
- No KYC, no platform fee, no custody.

You only ever pay the chain.
